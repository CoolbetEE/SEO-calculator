Step 3: Select Market

Choose the market where you’re targeting your audience.

Step 4: Select Position

Choose the search engine position you expect to rank at (e.g., Position 1 has a 30% click-through rate).

How We Calculate Your Revenue

We use the following steps to estimate your revenue:

  • Traffic: Monthly Search Volume × Click-Through Rate (CTR) for your selected position.
  • Conversions: Traffic × Conversion Rate (specific to your market).
  • Revenue: Conversions × Average Order Value (NGR or Turnover, depending on your selection).
  • Quarterly: Monthly results are multiplied by 3 to estimate a quarter (3 months).
